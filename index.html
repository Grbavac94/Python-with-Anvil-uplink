Projekt AdvIser kao što i samo ime kaže je vodič za planinare(početnike,experte), avanturiste, bicikliste, motoriste, vozače osobnih automobila, pješaka, za ljude koji žele naučiti više.
Projekt bi trebao olakšati svima koji se nalaze prvi puta na nekom području a da žele posjetiti bilo koje mjesto da ih AI provede kroz povijest toga mjesta pouzdane ceste kojima se može kretati, treba li oprema na tome području koliko je dugačak put, ima li nekih prekrasnih pogleda tim putem , jel zahtjevan put. Krenuti ćemo sa web stranicom da bi provjerili kako bi to moglo funkcionirati i kako ćemo to dalje razvijati. Poslije web stranice napraviti ćemo aplikaciju za taj AdvIser. Mogućnosti su vrlo široke i ima ih beskonačno zbog toga što je AI u neprestanom razvoju.


The AdvIser project, as the name suggests, is a guide for hikers (beginners, experts), adventurers, cyclists, motorcyclists, car drivers, pedestrians, for people who want to learn more. The project should make it easier for anyone who is in an area for the first time and who wants to visit any place to have the AI ​​guide them through the history of that place, reliable roads that can be navigated, do you need equipment in that area, how long is the road, are there any beautiful look at that road, is it a demanding road. We will start with the website to see how it might work and how we will develop it further. After the website, we will make an application for that AdvIser. The possibilities are very wide and endless because AI is constantly evolving.
